How to Play

All 4 snakes move simultaneously on the same arena.

Eat food to grow longer and earn 10 points.

Die if you hit a wall, yourself, or another snake.

The last snake alive wins the round!

Press Space to restart after game over.

About

4 Snake Royale

A 4-player competitive snake game built with Phaser 3 and TypeScript. Play with up to 4 players on the same keyboard!
