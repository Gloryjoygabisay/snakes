How to Play

When your snake dies, a logic challenge pops up. Answer correctly to revive and keep playing! Answer wrong or run out of time and it's game over.

Touch & hold the screen — the snake curves toward your finger. Move your finger to steer. Desktop: use WASD or arrow keys.

Space to restart after game over.

💀 Second Chance! Answer to stay alive — 6 seconds!
